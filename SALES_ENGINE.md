## Who You're Selling To

**Ideal client profile:**
- 10–200 employees
- Uses 3+ software systems that don't talk to each other
- Has operational pain — people asking the same questions repeatedly
- Owner/operator who values efficiency over buzzwords
- Revenue $1M–$50M (can afford $15K–$35K, cares about ROI)

**Industries where this hits hardest:**
- Logistics / trucking / freight (dispatchers, tracking, customer updates)
- Construction / contracting (job sites, materials, subs, invoices)
- Manufacturing (inventory, suppliers, production schedules)
- Professional services (client records, billing, project management)
- Healthcare practices (patient records, scheduling, insurance)
- Real estate / property management (tenants, maintenance, listings)

**Red flags (don't waste time):**
- "We're exploring AI" with no operational pain
- Wants a chatbot on their website (different product)
- No existing software systems (nothing to integrate)
- Expects free consulting or endless discovery

## The Discovery Call Script

**Goal:** Get them talking about pain. Don't pitch.

1. "Tell me about your business — what do you do, who does it, what tools do you use?"
2. "What questions does your team ask each other 20 times a day?"
3. "Where does information live? If I asked 'what's the status of order X,' who knows?"
4. "What's the cost of not knowing? Has a dropped ball ever cost you?"
5. "If you could ask one question and get an instant, accurate answer from all your systems at once — what would it be?"

**Close:** "I can build that. It takes about two weeks to prototype, costs [price], and you'll have something working by [date]. Want me to send over a one-page scope?"

## Deliverables Checklist

### Discovery ($2,500)
- [ ] 2-hour systems audit (recorded, with permission)
- [ ] Systems architecture diagram (hand-drawn is fine — authenticity beats polish)
- [ ] Top 3–5 use cases ranked by expected ROI
- [ ] Integration feasibility assessment (which systems can be connected, which can't)
- [ ] Proposed timeline and Full Integration quote

### Full Integration ($15K–$35K)
- [ ] All Discovery deliverables
- [ ] Working AI interface (private chat, web-based)
- [ ] Up to 5 systems connected (APIs, webhooks, DB queries)
- [ ] Retrieval architecture (RAG pipeline, vector store, or direct query routing)
- [ ] Prompt architecture with business-specific guardrails
- [ ] Team onboarding session (1 hour)
- [ ] 4 weeks of iteration based on actual usage
- [ ] Documentation (what's connected, how to add new data sources)
- [ ] Handoff training for any technical staff

### Ongoing ($3K–$8K/mo)
- [ ] Monthly strategy session (30 min)
- [ ] Continuous refinement based on usage patterns
- [ ] New integrations as needed (one per month typical)
- [ ] Priority support (4-hour response during business hours)
- [ ] Quarterly ROI review

## Objection Handling

**"We already have [ERP/CRM/AI tool]."**
"Great — that means you have systems worth connecting. My job is to make them talk to each other so your team doesn't have to manually translate between them."

**"Can't we just use ChatGPT?"**
"ChatGPT doesn't know your customer history, your inventory levels, or your internal procedures. I make an AI that DOES — it pulls from your actual systems, not the internet."

**"That's expensive."**
"Compared to what? Your operations manager spending 10 hours a week answering the same questions? A wrong answer that costs you a client? An ERP implementation that takes 18 months and costs $200K? This is the cheapest thing you'll buy this year that actually changes how your business runs."

**"We need to think about it."**
"Totally fair. In the meantime, can I send you the Discovery option? It's $2,500, takes two hours of your time, and you walk away with a concrete roadmap — whether or not you hire me for the build."

Common Questions

What systems can you integrate?

Anything with an API or database: Salesforce, HubSpot, Zendesk, Shopify, QuickBooks, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, custom internal tools, SQL databases, spreadsheets, email, Slack, and more. If it stores data, we can connect it.

Is my data secure?

Your data stays in your systems. The AI queries your data on-demand — we don't store copies or train models on your information. We can deploy entirely within your infrastructure if needed.

How long until we see value?

Most clients have a working prototype within 2 weeks. The Discovery session alone usually identifies quick wins you can implement immediately. Full integration typically delivers measurable ROI within the first month.

Do we need technical staff to maintain this?

No. The system is designed to be used by your existing team through a simple chat interface. We handle all technical maintenance. If you have engineers, they can extend the system — but you don't need them.
